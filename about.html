Meet the people behind the curtain and learn about our home.

Opera for Everyone

The EveryVoice Emerald Coast Arts was founded on a simple belief: that the joy of live opera should be available to every member of our community — not just those with access to major metropolitan theatres.

Since our first production, we have grown into a beloved institution that trains young vocalists, welcomes guest artists, and fills our Main Stage with world-class performances that rival those of much larger companies.

Whether you're a lifelong opera devotee or attending your very first performance, we welcome you with open arms and full voices.
